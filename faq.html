General & Licensing

Is this a subscription?

expand_more
No. WorkNoted is a one-time purchase. We believe you should own your software. One payment gives you a lifetime license for the current major version.

Who is the target audience?

expand_more
WorkNoted is optimized for students, freelancers, and researchers who need a clean, distraction-free environment and complete control over their intellectual property.

What operating systems are supported?

expand_more
Currently, WorkNoted is available for Windows only. We are focusing on delivering the best experience on the PC platform before expanding to other systems.
lock

Technical & Privacy

Where is my data stored?

expand_more
Your data is stored 100% locally on your computer. You have full control over the database files—you can move, backup, or delete them whenever you wish.

Can I attach files to my notes?

expand_more
Yes. You can attach PDFs, documents, and other files directly to your notes to keep all your resources organized in one place.

How can I export my work?

expand_more
You can export your notes to PDF or TXT formats with a single click, allowing you to share your work or use it in other applications easily.
