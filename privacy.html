Privacy Policy

info

In short: NewAgent is Local-First.

We do not collect your notes, files, or personal data. Everything stays on your PC. The only data that leaves your device is anonymized crash reports if you opt-in.

This Privacy Policy outlines NewAgent’s commitment to a local-first architecture. Unlike cloud-based tools, WorkNoted is designed to operate offline-first, meaning your data is stored locally on your hard drive.

Data Collection

We collect zero user-generated content. Your notes, tasks, and project files are never transmitted to our servers. We do not have access to your database password or encryption keys.

Local Storage

All application data is stored in a SQLite database located at ~/Documents/WorkNoted/ by default. You have full control over this file. You can back it up, move it, or delete it at any time.

Third Parties

We use a limited set of third-party services strictly for payment processing (Stripe) and optional crash reporting (Sentry). These services do not have access to your notes content.

Advantages of Local-First Privacy Protection

Zero Attack Surface

By choosing an offline-first workspace, you eliminate the primary target for data breaches. Your intellectual property is never exposed to central cloud vulnerabilities.

GDPR-Compliant by Design

Because we never collect your personal data, you have absolute control over your information. There is no need for complex data processing agreements because the data stays on your hardware.
